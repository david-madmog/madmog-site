ABOUT TAINTED HORIZON

Tainted Horizon is my first attempt to make a game. I'm using Gamemaker Pro to do it. The goal of this project is to gain experience in the area of developing games and larger programming projects.
The goal is to create a hardcore pixelart rougelike with randomized levels and loot. At the moment, most sprites are placeholders until I get around to making proper ones!
