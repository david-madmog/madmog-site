ABOUT SUPER TERRARIA WORLD

Super Terraria World (STW) is a standalone role-playing mod, which will be kept up-to-date with any official release of Terraria. The primary goal of this mod is to bring a MMORPG element to this wonderful game. Currently, the devs are only releasing Single Player Alpha versions of STW. This is so they can focus all of their effort on creating plenty of quality content for you us to enjoy.

Although I have not worked the actual game, I created the installer for it, which is currently only for Windows. I am working on porting the installer to Mac and Linux using Mono.
